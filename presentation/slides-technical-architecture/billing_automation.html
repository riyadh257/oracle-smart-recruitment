Pay-for-Performance Billing
Billing Models
Subscription
Fixed monthly or annual fee for platform access with unlimited job postings and candidate matching
Performance-Based
Pay only for qualified applications and scheduled interviews — aligns cost with hiring success
Hybrid
Combination of base subscription fee plus performance-based charges for premium features
