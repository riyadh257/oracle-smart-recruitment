## AI Adoption in Recruitment

### Current Adoption Rates
- **67%** of organizations now use some form of AI in their recruitment process
- **43%** of organizations used AI for HR tasks in 2025 (up from 26% in 2024)
- **75%** of HR professionals report AI as their top technology investment

### Key Benefits Reported
- **73%** said AI can help match candidates to other roles in the organization
- **51%** of TA professionals believe AI can improve quality of hire
- **+9%** improvement for companies using AI-Assisted Messaging
